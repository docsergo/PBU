We are all humiliated by the sudden discovery of a fact which has existed very comfortably and perhaps been staring at us in private while we have been making up our world entirely without it.— George Eliot, Middlemarch

We've been thrown into a new paradigm. You already know this. What you might not know yet is what to do with it — or where to start.

Problems B Us is not a guide. It will not prepare you for possible futures, because the preparation of students for possible futures is, as we've come to understand it, a psychological fantasy. What it will do is sit with you in the problem for a while, and occasionally stand up in it, and occasionally dance.

What kind of problem are you sitting with right now?
